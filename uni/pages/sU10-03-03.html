unit 10 part III

 

 

objective ?

To choose a collection of objects, things, documents, etc. and design an archival system for it.

 

outcome ?

I attempted to archive browsing behaviour on the internet and to visualise a user's search history in a meaningful way. I attempted this as a browser extension and a feature extension of a digital product called Are.na.

week 02 getting feedback and incorporating it into our work

29.02.18 - 08.03.18

 

 

Prototype below

 

Creating a concept for a browser extension to have a better overview of what kind of tabs are currently open. Data collected and shown is: Time spent on the page, time the page has been open and connections of content to other pages.

 

 

 

 

Feedback

 

Safari already has a pretty great function where you can zoom out and see all your open tabs in a grid. My extension does more or less the same but doesn't display the data as cleanly and coherently.
