S E A S O N S   G R E E T I N G S   |  proposal for digital posters

What was the objective of this project?

Design the 2017 festive digital campaign visual for UAL. This can be a static or animated image, and will be used on all UAL websites, email campaigns and college screens.

 

What did I do?

I wanted to create something playful and festive but still relatively neutral as to not alienate any particular beliefs or traditions. I experimented with interactive type and gravitated towards outcomes where the type seemed almost like objects, such as snow or presents falling / dancing around you.
