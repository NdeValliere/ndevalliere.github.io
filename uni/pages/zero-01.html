about this project

Our intent is to consider and position publishing in a post-digital discourse. This will be done by creating a new system of publishing that takes into account new ways of production and dissemination methods brought by the internet.

We want to look at publishing, curation, digital vs analogue, personalisation, print-on-demand, archiving, dissemination, durability, collective input, collaborative/ democratic translation and methods of reinterpretation.

By linking digital and analogue publishing platforms, and reconsidering the position of the editor as a curator, we wish to highlight the new curatorial ethos in publishing: “The act of selection has replaced that of production” Dorothea Van Hantelmann.

Reconsider, explore, question the way we organize information for publishing, while questioning methods of mass-production and variability. How can we push reproduction? How unique can it be until it is not relatable anymore?
