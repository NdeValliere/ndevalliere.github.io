unit 10 part I

 

 

objective ?

Create a conditional design system that results in a series of outcomes.

 

outcome ?

I created a digitally explorable environment based on Google Maps. The user input, their choice of where to "walk" within Google Maps around Kings Cross dictates what the drawing machine will create. The system is the Google Maps street view environment, the code mapping their journey simplifying it into a series of coordinates and the drawing machine outputting it into a piece of ephemera of their journey.

final outcomes

 

 

 

My initial concept can be seen above: I take screenshots every 10s of the user's journey and have the drawing machine draw those series of images in a comic format. After receiving comments that what I was doing was confusing, I tried to keep everything as simple as possible.

 

I scrapped the idea of creating a world based on a comic story + George Orwell's novel 1984. I took data from Open Street Maps on the Kings Cross area, translated that into a 3d model and replaced the model I had created before. The user input stays the same: It is their personal exploration of the digital world. With a C# script I track their movements around the digital world and have them outputted by the drawing machine as a series of x, y, z coordinates in a long scroll.
