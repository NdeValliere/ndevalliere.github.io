D A N C E   |  digital and physical posters

What was the objective of this project?

Create a set of promotional material that communicates the concept of either Design for dance or Dance Lab. Use design methods to capture and communicate movement/motion/dance which can be inclusive of drawing, painting, coding, photography, printmaking etc. and specific text and logos which will need to be applied to each of the posters.

 

What did I do?

I focused on trying to recreate movement with type similar to what I tried to do for the Christmas card campaign but tried to make it a little more abstract than the previous outcomes. I tried to experiment with as many different digital processes and mediums as possible to stretch what is possible and expand my personal knowledge.
