R H O N D A  |  a drawing machine

What was the objective of this project?

Create a bridge between analogue and digital design tools.

 

What did I do?

With the help of Jaap de Maat and assistance of Michael McShane, we built a drawing machine following the open source instructions created by Sandy Noble.
