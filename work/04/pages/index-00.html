Publishing platforms

Self-directed brief, collaboration with Inês Silva and Dagnė Petraitytė

We created a publishing platform where the presence of physical and digital components complement each other: We made the first issue of a magazine that exists in a physical and a digital format. Our intent is to consider and position publishing in a post-­digital discourse. We took into account new structures, ways of production and dissemination methods brought by the internet (the possibility of digital circulation and accessibility) while at the same time valuing the printed format.

In its' digital format the magazine issues are distributed via a website. They are downloadable as ZIP files and expand into a series of folders.

In its' physical format the magazine is currently distributed by us upon personal request. These requests can be made via email, details of which are available to the public on the aforementioned website.

Generative narratives

Personal project, collaboration with Jaap de Maat.

I translated an old personal comic (awarded 3rd place in an international Sci-Fi competition out of 800+ entries) into an explorable digital environment.

The player's experience/journey through the maze is documented through screenshots. These images are collated into a typical comic page format and drawn by a machine.
